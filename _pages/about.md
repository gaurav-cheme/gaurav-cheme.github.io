Welcome to our research group at IIT Indore! We use computational and theoretical tools to uncover the role of phase separation of proteins/RNA/DNA on biochemical reactions. We use computer simulations to uncover rich polymer physics associated with biomolecular condensates, both under equilibrium and non-equilibrium conditions. We also engineer better enzymes for efficient biofuel production using computational tools.
